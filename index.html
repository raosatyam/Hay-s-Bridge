Instructions

Hays Bridge Experiment

    STEP 1: Pick up three resistances, a battery, a inductance, a capcitor and a galvanometer from the toolbox.
    STEP 2: Connect all component as shown in the figure to form a Hays Bridge Circuit.
    STEP 3: After making the circuit click on check circuit button to check the connections
    STEP 4: Now Enter the the value of R2, R3, Capacitance, Volatge and Frequency and try to make deflection in meter close to zero.
    STEP 5: Calculate the value of inductance using the formula given and check the value using the check button.
    STEP 6: Enter the value untill the bridge gets balanced.
    STEP 7: Click on submit button to add it into observation table.
    STEP 8: Repeat the Experiment for new generated value of inductance.
